About US

RaktaConnect was born from a simple but powerful belief that no one should lose their life because the right blood wasn't available at the right time. We are a digital platform built to bridge the gap between blood donors, NGOs, hospitals, blood banks, and corporate partners, making blood donation more accessible, organized, and impactful than ever before.

Our Mission

To create a digitally inclusive ecosystem where finding and donating blood is seamless, transparent, and rewarding -> for everyone, regardless of language, ability, or location
